Franklin University Faculty – Research, Presentations, and Global Interaction

Our experienced faculty members gain the input of industry experts to develop courses with relevant concepts our students use right away – at work and in life.

Beyond developing and teaching courses, faculty members are actively involved in the community, conducting research, giving presentations, and offering expert advice and services to media outlets and professional and charitable organizations around the world.
